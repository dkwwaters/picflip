PicFlip — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

The short version

PicFlip collects no data. None.

The full version

PicFlip converts images entirely on your Mac. Your photos never leave your computer: the app has no servers, no analytics, no tracking, no advertising, and no third-party services of any kind. It makes no network connections.

When you import photos from a connected iPhone or iPad, the transfer happens directly over the USB cable between your device and your Mac, using Apple's system frameworks. PicFlip does not see, store, or transmit anything beyond writing the files to the folder you choose.

Settings you choose in the app (such as your preferred output format) are stored locally on your Mac and nowhere else.
